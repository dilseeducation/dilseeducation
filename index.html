Dil Se Education
DilSe (From The Heart) we believe that every child in the world deserves access to quality education. Our mission is to make profound and sustaining difference in the lives of children in our country. At Dil Se, we support programs that improve the physical, emotional and educational lives of underprivileged and at-risk children.
